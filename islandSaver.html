Plunder Panic

Plunder Panic is an arcade style action platformer for up to twelve players. With four ways to win, your team of plundering pirates must work together to kill the enemy captain, gather gold, row the rowboat and gather gold.


Plunder Panic has been in development by the GEL Lab since 2017. It began as a fun project within the GEL and quickly took off as one of MSU's staple games. In 2017 Plunder Panic attended IndieCade and won the audience choice award. Development on Plunder Panic continued with a large graphics overhaul and the aim to add AI bots as well as port the game to the Nintendo Switch. At IndieCade 2019 Plunder Panic was asked to be part of the IndieCade esports showcase. Plunder Panic is planned to release in 2020.
