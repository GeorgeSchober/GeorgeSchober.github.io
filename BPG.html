Brain Powered Games Africa

Brain Powered Game Africa is a suite of games designed to help children that have been mentally stunted from HIV and malaria.


The original suite of BPG games was developed almost ten years ago. I was asked to work on revamping the original suite of games in preparation for use in a research study in Uganda and Malawi as well as help create a new game, Village Builder, to add to the set. Village Builder is a real time strategy game focused on improving the executive functions of affected children. In September 2019 we traveled to Kampala in order to playtest the revamped games and train the local researches in conducting sessions with the games. Over a period of a week, we continued to improve the games and ensure they were in the best shape possible before the start of the study.
