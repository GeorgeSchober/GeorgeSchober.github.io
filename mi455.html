Art of Conjury

Art of Conjury is a 2D sidecrolling puzzle game. You must conjure up your three different objects, a boulder, a crate and a pillar in order to traverse the surrounding environment.

Reflection

Reflection is a 2D side scroller puzzle game with two dimensions, one dimension is normal while the other is the environment reflected in the water. The environments are inverse, when you exists in one you will not in the other. You must manipulate the environments destroying and moving boxes in one to affect the other.
